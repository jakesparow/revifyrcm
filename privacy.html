01 Scope & who we are

This policy is published by RevifyRCM, Inc. ("RevifyRCM", "we", "us"), a Delaware corporation headquartered in Bastrop, Texas. It explains how we handle information when you visit revifyrcm.com, request a demo, or use our services as a covered entity, business associate, or end-user.

Throughout this document, "Customer" means a healthcare provider organization that has signed a Master Services Agreement (MSA) and Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with us. "End-user" means a Customer's authorized employee accessing our portal.

03 How we use information

  • Provide the service — eligibility, coding, claim submission, denial appeals, AR follow-up, payment posting, reporting.
  • Operate & secure — authentication, fraud detection, audit logging, performance monitoring.
  • Support — respond to tickets, train end-users, investigate incidents.
  • Improve — measure agent accuracy and throughput; improve models on de-identified, aggregated data only.
  • Sales & marketing — answer inquiries, send the Monday Read-out newsletter (with consent), conduct webinars.

We do not sell PHI. We do not use PHI for cross-customer marketing, lead scoring, or AI training without contractual permission.

04 PHI & HIPAA

RevifyRCM is a HIPAA Business Associate. Before any PHI flows to us, a Business Associate Agreement is executed. The BAA governs use, disclosure, breach notification, and termination. Our standard BAA is reviewable on request.

Minimum necessary

Agents and humans access only the PHI needed for the assigned task. Role-based access controls and just-in-time elevation are enforced at the database, application, and network layers.

Breach notification

We notify affected Customers within 48 hours of confirmed unauthorized PHI disclosure — well inside the 60-day HIPAA standard. Notification includes nature of breach, PHI involved, mitigation, and remediation timeline.

06 Retention

  • PHI & claim data — retained for the length of the MSA + 7 years (or per state law, whichever is longer), then destroyed.
  • Account data — retained while account active; deleted within 90 days of termination.
  • Marketing data — 24 months from last interaction unless you opt out sooner.
  • System logs — 13 months for security and audit; logs do not contain PHI.

07 Security

We maintain a written information security program aligned to SOC 2 Type II, the HIPAA Security Rule, and HITRUST CSF v11. Highlights:

  • Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Customer-specific encryption keys, rotated annually
  • Mandatory MFA, hardware tokens for engineering production access
  • Continuous vulnerability scanning & quarterly penetration testing
  • Background checks for all personnel with PHI access
  • 24/7 security operations center

08 Your rights

If you are a patient

RevifyRCM holds PHI as a Business Associate. To exercise your HIPAA rights (access, amendment, accounting of disclosures, restriction, confidential communications, complaint), contact your healthcare provider directly — they are the covered entity. We will assist them in fulfilling your request.

If you are a website visitor (CCPA/CPRA, GDPR)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we collected directly from you (e.g. demo form submissions). Email privacy@revifyrcm.com; we respond within 30 days.

09 Cookies & tracking

We use a small set of cookies. The portal sets only strictly-necessary cookies (session, CSRF). The marketing site uses analytics cookies that you can decline via the consent banner. We honor Global Privacy Control signals.

10 AI agents & training data

Our LLM-powered agents run on enterprise tiers with zero retention and contractually-disabled training. PHI is never used to train foundation models. We may use de-identified, aggregated outcome data (denial codes, recovery rates, response times) to improve our own internal models — never the underlying provider's identifying information.

In one sentence

If a model can be retrained or memorize, your PHI doesn't go near it.

11 Children's privacy

Our services are sold to healthcare organizations, not consumers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through our marketing site. Pediatric PHI is handled under the Customer's BAA.

12 International transfers

Production PHI is stored and processed exclusively in U.S. AWS regions. Our Chennai, India engineering team accesses development and metrics environments only — not production PHI — via U.S.-hosted bastions with full session recording and DLP. Standard Contractual Clauses are in place where required.

13 Changes to this policy

We post a notice at the top of this page when we make material changes and email Customers' designated privacy contacts at least 30 days before changes take effect. The version number above always reflects the current document.
