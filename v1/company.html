Operating principles

Five non-negotiables.

01

Outcome over hours.

We don't bill for FTEs, claims, or seats. If your collections don't move, neither does our invoice. Pricing is the strongest forcing function for quality, so we wrote it into our DNA.

02

Agent-first, human-finished.

Agents do the volume. Humans handle exceptions, judgment calls, and provider relationships. Both work the same portal. Both are accountable to the same SLA.

03

Compounding knowledge.

Every claim improves the next one. Every denial trains the appeal. The data your last vendor threw away is the data we capture and reuse — under your control, on your tenant.

04

Open ledger.

Every agent prompt, model, decision, and action is logged in your portal — replayable, exportable, defensible. No black boxes. Auditors love us.

05

One throat to choke.

Coding, eligibility, denials, AR, patient pay, contracting — under one roof, one P&L, one SLA. Stop coordinating five vendors. Start running one team.

06

U.S.-grounded operations.

Engineered and operated from the United States. Provider-state-licensed humans where the work calls for them. No tribal knowledge attriting offshore.
