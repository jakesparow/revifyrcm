Founder & President

Amanda Perez

Austin native. Twenty-one years leading revenue cycle for primary care, ortho, and specialty groups across Texas and the Southeast. Co-founded an offshore RCM company before realizing the future of the industry wasn't in cheaper labor — it was in agents that don't sleep.

"I spent a decade scaling a 600-person billing floor. Then I watched a single coding agent do the work of forty of my best people, faster and cleaner. That was the moment Revify started."

Amanda founded RevifyRCM in 2024 in Bastrop, Texas — twenty miles from Starlink HQ and the Boring Company. The pitch: an RCM operator built agent-first, with humans only where judgment matters, and pricing tied entirely to what gets posted to your bank.

She runs the operating side of the company: client relationships, payer escalations, and the every-Monday review that pulls every CFO read-out into one room. She speaks at HFMA and MGMA on the post-BPO operating model.

President · Innovation & Technology

Sriram Raghavan

Built the agent network from prompt to production. Specialist in payer-policy ML, large-scale claim adjudication, and the boring infrastructure that makes the magic work.

"A denial isn't a billing problem. It's a policy retrieval problem with a deadline. Once you frame it that way, the entire stack rewrites itself."

Sriram leads engineering and applied research. He architected the agent runtime, the payer-policy retrieval system that powers Denial Triage, and the FHIR ingest layer that lets us read from any major EHR.

Before Revify: ML at a top-five payer, where he saw firsthand which adjudication levers actually move dollars. He's the reason every agent action ends up in your portal as an auditable, replayable trace — not a black box.

Director · Client Relationships

Greg Garza

A decade-plus on the front lines of RCM. Photographic memory of payer policies and human-calculator precision on AR. The person every CFO wants on speed dial when a $3M claim is hanging in the wind.

"My job is to be the human you call when the agent escalates. By the time it gets to me, the work is already 90% done. I'm just here to close it."

Greg owns the service relationship with every Revify client. He runs the Monday CFO read-outs alongside Amanda, leads exception triage for high-value claims, and trains every new ops pod that joins the company.

His background spans hospital billing, multi-specialty group ops, and payer-side appeals — which is why he can read an EOB in twelve seconds and tell you exactly where the dollar went sideways.

Director · Global Operations

Saravanan K.

Runs the Chennai operations center — the human escalation layer behind every agent action. Twelve years in end-to-end RCM across Epic, Athena, AdvancedMD, Tebra, eMDs, Valant, and Mahler, with a track record of cutting denials 25% and lifting clean-claim rates above 95%.

"The agents do the volume. We do the calls payers don't want to take. That's the partnership."

Saravanan owns global operations for RevifyRCM — the 24-hour exception desk that picks up wherever an agent escalates, the QA layer that audits agent decisions, and the training engine that keeps every reasoning trace HIPAA- and payer-policy-compliant.

His teams have driven measurable outcomes for behavioral health, primary care, and multispecialty groups: 20% reduction in days-in-AR, 15–20% lift in collections, denial rates cut by a quarter — all while compounding the agent training data underneath.
