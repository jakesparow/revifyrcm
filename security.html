Controls,
not slogans.

01

Encryption everywhere

AES-256 at rest. TLS 1.3 in transit. Customer-specific keys in AWS KMS, rotated annually. Field-level encryption for the most sensitive PHI columns.

02

Identity & access

SSO/SAML required for all customer end-users. Hardware-token MFA for engineering. Just-in-time elevation with full session recording. Quarterly access reviews.

03

Network & isolation

Per-customer database schemas. VPC-isolated production. No flat networks. Egress allowlists. AWS PrivateLink for sensitive integrations.

04

Monitoring & logging

Every agent run, API call, and data access is logged with user, IP, and timestamp. Logs are write-once, retained 13 months, and exportable to your SIEM.

05

Vulnerability management

Continuous SAST/DAST in CI. Quarterly third-party penetration tests. Public bug-bounty program at HackerOne (private invitation tier).

06

Personnel

Background checks, drug screens, and signed confidentiality agreements before any PHI access. Annual HIPAA, security, and phishing training. Acceptable-use policy enforced via MDM.

07

Vendor management

All subprocessors are reviewed annually. Customers get 30 days' notice before any new PHI subprocessor is added. Subprocessor list at privacy.html#share.

08

Resilience

Multi-AZ production with automatic failover. Daily encrypted backups, 35-day point-in-time recovery. Annual disaster recovery test with documented RTO 4h / RPO 1h.
