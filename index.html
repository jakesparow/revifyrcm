Why Revify

Healthcare doesn't have a billing problem.
It has a workforce problem.

For thirty years the answer has been the same: hire more people. Open a captive in Manila, route claims to Chennai, layer a clearinghouse on top, and pretend the result is technology. It isn't. It's labor arbitrage with a portal.

What changed in 2024 is that a model can read a payer policy faster than a human, draft an appeal letter that cites the LCD it was denied against, and post 835 remits at 3am with full lineage. Not "AI assist." Real work, owned end to end. Audited.

RevifyRCM is what happens when you build that workforce from a clean sheet — with hospital operators, healthcare engineers, and a coding team that has read every CARC code since 2009 still in the room. We get paid only when you collect. That alignment changes everything.

I came up in operations. I have stared at aging reports at 11pm. The first time I watched our denial agent draft an appeal — citing the right LCD, in the patient's language — I knew the work I had been doing for fifteen years was about to change. So I quit, and built it.
Amanda Perez · Founder & President · 15 years RCM operator · Bastrop, TX
